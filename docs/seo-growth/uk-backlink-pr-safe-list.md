# Safe UK Backlink and PR List (No Spam, No PBN)

## Standards
- Only relevant, editorial, and brand-safe placements.
- No paid link farms, no spun content, no hidden anchor schemes.
- Focus on trust, citations, and qualified referral traffic.

## Priority outreach types
1. UK business and startup media
- founder story, delivery frameworks, case-study insights.

2. SaaS, ecommerce, and dev communities
- practical guides with examples and technical takeaways.

3. Industry podcasts and webinars
- repurpose appearance into article + social proof link.

4. Partner ecosystem pages
- stack partners (Shopify/WooCommerce/analytics tools).

5. Local UK business chambers and networks
- local trust + geographic relevance for UK entity SEO.

## Suggested target categories
- UK digital business magazines
- UK startup publications
- technical marketing newsletters
- ecommerce operations blogs
- CRM and automation communities
- trusted B2B directories with editorial review

## Anchor strategy
- 70% branded/naked anchors
- 20% partial topical anchors
- 10% generic anchors
- Avoid repeated exact-match keyword anchors.

## Monthly target (safe pace)
- 2 to 4 editorial links
- 3 to 6 quality citations
- 1 to 2 PR mentions or interviews

## Reporting fields
- source domain
- domain relevance
- link type (follow/nofollow/mention)
- target URL
- anchor text
- acquisition date
- referral clicks
