# 90-Day UK SEO Growth Roadmap (No UI Changes)

## Days 1-30: foundation and cluster execution
- Publish/refresh pillar page (`/uk-growth-hub`) and 5 support blogs.
- Ensure each blog links to service + pricing/contact + portfolio + pillar.
- Complete GBP and first-wave citation setup.
- Validate GA4 events and mark conversions in GA4 admin.

## Days 31-60: authority and trust
- Publish 4 new buyer-intent blog articles mapped to services.
- Add 2 case-study style portfolio updates with measurable outcomes.
- Acquire 3 to 5 editorial/citation links from safe UK sources.
- Start monthly brand mention and PR outreach cadence.

## Days 61-90: scale and optimization
- Expand clusters by sector (healthcare, law firms, ecommerce, B2B).
- Improve pages with high impressions but low CTR (titles/descriptions).
- Build second wave of links to service pages and pillar page.
- Review conversion paths:
  - lead form completion rate
  - meeting booking rate
  - checkout start rate

## KPI targets
- impressions growth
- non-brand clicks growth
- conversion events growth (`generate_lead`, `schedule_meeting`, `begin_checkout`)
- increased indexed high-intent pages
